How People Build Wealth

Building wealth is a long-term process that requires smart decisions and consistency. Most successful investors focus on saving, investing, and managing risk over time.

💰 Save First

Always save part of your income before spending.

📈 Invest Early

Starting early gives your money more time to grow.

🔁 Be Consistent

Invest regularly instead of trying to time the market.

⚖️ Manage Risk

Spread your investments to avoid big losses.

Example of Wealth Growth

If someone saves and invests money every month, their wealth can grow over time. This is called compound growth, where money earns more money.

For example, investing small amounts consistently can grow into a large amount over several years. This is why patience is very important in investing.
