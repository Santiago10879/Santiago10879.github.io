Different Forms of Wealth

Wealth is not only about money. It can also include things people own and skills that help them earn money.

Main Types

  1. Financial Wealth
  2. Physical Wealth
  3. Human Capital
  4. Social Wealth

Description List

Financial Wealth
Money and investments like stocks.
Physical Wealth
Things people own such as houses or land.
Human Capital
Skills and education that help someone earn money.
