What is Wealth?

Wealth is the value of everything a person owns. This includes money, property, and investments. Wealth is important because it gives people financial security and allows them to handle emergencies, achieve goals, and enjoy life.

Many people think wealth is just about having a lot of money, but it also includes having valuable assets and making smart financial decisions over time. Building wealth takes patience, discipline, and knowledge.

💼 Earn

Earn money through jobs, side hustles, or businesses.

📈 Invest

Grow your money by investing in stocks and assets.

💰 Save

Save money to prepare for the future and emergencies.
