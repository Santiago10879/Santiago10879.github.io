What is Wealth?

Wealth is the total value of everything a person owns, including money, property, and investments. It provides financial security and helps people achieve long-term goals.

Building wealth requires time, discipline, and smart decisions. It is not about getting rich quickly, but about growing your resources steadily.

Why Wealth Matters

  • ✔ Ability to handle emergencies
  • ✔ More life opportunities
  • ✔ Less stress about money
